WHAT INSPIRES ME?

Here, you can find a collection of pieces that inspire me, or songs that I like to listen to.


IMAGES

I've been a fan of video games since I was young, and one of my favourite things about older video games like Super Mario World, or Pokemon Blue and Red, and even games from the past 15 years like Pokemon Diamond and Pearl or Hyper Light Drifter is the art style. These games all hold a very particular 8-bit style. Something about it is just really fascinating to me.

MUSIC
