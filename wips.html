WORKS IN PROGRESS

There are plenty of things that I'm working on all at once and for those of you who are interested, this is the page that shows all of my current works in progress, whether it be a design, a video, or something different altogether! Take a look, and see if something catches your interest!

STARFALL

Starfall is a pseudo-anime style show that follows the story of a girl who is tasked with venturing out on a dangerous journey, that could cost her life. Currently, me and my small team (my friends) are in pre-production. Pre-production of any direct-to-animation series is character design, story concept, story boarding, music, etc. This may not come to fruition for at least another year or so as my team and I are primarily all students so stay tuned for more news and information

Off-Topic

Off-topic is a series of song artwork used as part of an EP release. The off-topic track artwork is being designed to appeal to a younger audience, preferrably high-schoolers, as well as convey the idea that you can do whatever you want if you try
